## 📺 About the Series
**Bloodhounds** is a South Korean action-drama series released on Netflix.  
It follows two young boxers who join forces with a kind-hearted lender to fight against dangerous loan sharks exploiting innocent people.

---

## 🎭 Main Characters
- **Kim Geon-woo** – A talented and disciplined boxer with strong morals  
- **Hong Woo-jin** – His funny yet loyal best friend and partner  
- **President Choi** – A generous businessman who helps victims of debt  

---

## ⚡ Genre
- Action  
- Crime  
- Drama  

---

## ⭐ Why I Like This Series
- 💥 Intense and realistic fight scenes  
- 🤝 Strong friendship and teamwork  
- ❤️ Emotional and meaningful story  
- ⚖️ Shows real-life issues like debt and injustice  

---

## 🎬 Favorite Scene
One of my favorite scenes is when Geon-woo and Woo-jin fight side by side against powerful enemies.  
It clearly shows their trust, teamwork, and determination to protect others.

---

## 📚 Lessons Learned

- Loyalty is important in friendships  
- Stand up against injustice and corruption  

---

## 🏁 Conclusion
Bloodhounds is not just an action series—it also teaches important life lessons about courage, kindness, and standing up for what is right.
